Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a popular italian dish notorious for its many different takes on the carb-heavy food.

Like said before, Pizza is an italian food originally made with a bread base, sauce, cheese, and some basil.

The picture you see above is a picture of a traditional Italian pizza which was created 100s of years ago.

This recipe is going to teach you how to make a certain type of Pizza called Detroit style. Detroit style pizza is notorious for being extremely calorie and carb dense. This recipe is going to be very difficult and time-consuming so buckle your seatbelts!

Ingredients

  • 500g of All-Purpose Flour
  • 300g of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Packets of Yeast
  • 200g of Mozarella
  • 50g of Basil

Instructions

  1. Mix in the Dough and Water and knead
  2. Add in Yeast and let it rest for 30m
  3. Quickly Re-knead the dough
  4. Add A thin layer of Tomatoe Sauce
  5. Add a thin layer of Cheese
  6. Alternate layers 4-5 times
  7. Preheat oven for 450F
  8. Bake Pizza for 40 minutes
  9. Remove Pizza from Oven
  10. Enjoy!
