Taco Recipe

Tacos are probably the most popular Mexican food. Tacos originates from Mexico.

Tacos was date back to the 1700s and was food to feed miners. Tacos were used because they were easy and cheap.

The image you see above is a rendition of the tacos eaten by miners back in the 1700s.

Buckle your seatbelts and get ready to create some of the greatest tacos you'll make in your life.

This recipe is going to teach you how to make the traditional taco which was made in the 1900s. This taco recipe is suprisingly simple.

Ingredients

  • 200g of All-Purpose Flour
  • 300g of Grass-Fed Beef
  • 300g of Water
  • 400g of Lettuce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Place the Tortillas on the Stove
  2. Cook Tortillas til charred
  3. Cook Beef in different pan
  4. Cook Beef for 20 minutes
  5. Add the Beef on the Tortilla
  6. Wrap the Beef in the Tortilla til halfway
  7. Sprinkle Lettuce in the Taco
  8. Add Salt and Pepper
  9. Throw away the other stuff since I'm making this recipe up
  10. Enjoy!
